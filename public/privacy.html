Privacy & Data Protection

How we collect, use and protect your personal information.

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Chinnyglo Agency ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

We are registered with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) and comply with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Candidate information: full name, email address, phone number, CV, professional qualifications, registration numbers (GMC, NMC), employment history, right to work documentation, date of birth, and references.
  • Client information: contact names, email addresses, phone numbers, facility details and staffing requirements.
  • Website usage data: IP address, browser type, pages visited and time spent on our site via standard analytics.

3. How We Use Your Data

We process personal data for the following purposes:

  • Matching candidates with suitable healthcare positions
  • Verifying professional qualifications, registrations and right to work
  • Communicating with candidates and clients about placements
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations (CQC, NHS frameworks)
  • Improving our website and services

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data under the following lawful bases: consent (where you have given it), contractual necessity (to provide our recruitment services), legal obligation (regulatory compliance), and legitimate interest (business operations that do not override your rights).

5. Data Sharing

We may share your data with:

  • NHS Trusts, private hospitals, nursing homes and care homes for placement purposes
  • Regulatory bodies (GMC, NMC) for compliance verification
  • Service providers who assist with our operations (hosting, email, analytics)

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

6. Data Retention

We retain candidate records for the duration of your engagement with us plus 6 years following the end of your last placement (in line with HMRC and regulatory requirements). Website usage data is retained for 26 months.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time
